Voice Diary Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Data Collection

Voice Diary is designed with your privacy in mind. Here's what we collect:

Speech Recognition

  • Voice Diary uses on-device speech recognition to transcribe your voice entries
  • All transcription happens locally on your device
  • No audio or text data is sent to external servers

Local Storage

  • All your diary entries are stored locally on your device
  • We do not have access to your personal entries

Subscriptions

  • Subscription purchases are processed through Apple's App Store
  • We do not collect or store payment information

Data Usage

Your data is used solely to provide the voice diary service:

  • Recording and transcribing voice entries
  • Saving your diary entries locally
  • Managing subscription status

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the App Store support channel.
